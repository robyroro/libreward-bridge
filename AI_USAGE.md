# AI usage disclosure

Substantial AI assistance was used in the development of LibreReward Bridge. Tools included Codex and Claude. Assistance covered implementation, documentation, test design and writing, and code/security review support.

The maintainers remain responsible for understanding, validating, licensing, operating, and maintaining all code and prose. Automated output does not constitute an independent security review, privacy review, legal advice, or regulatory approval. AI-assisted changes must meet the same tests, review, provenance, and contribution requirements as human-written changes.

The repository retains earlier attribution and prompt/output provenance where available in `docs/GENAI_USAGE.md` and `docs/nlnet/GENAI_PROPOSAL_LOG.md`. No fake or reconstructed prompt history should be added. Future AI-assisted grant text should retain the actual prompt, generated output, human revisions, and disclosure evidence when a funder requires them.

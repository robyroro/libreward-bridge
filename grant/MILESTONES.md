| Milestone | Target | Objective evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| M1: upstream integration contract | Month 2 | Published compatibility decisions, upstream questions/links, stable adapter tests, unknown-outcome matrix |
| M2: interoperability testbed | Month 4 | Reproducible valueless environment, sanitized machine-readable evidence, failure/restart matrix |
| M3: independent review/remediation | Month 6 | Third-party reports or attestations, issue dispositions, regression tests; unresolved risk explicit |
| M4: reusable integration kit | Month 7 | Versioned SDK, contract conformance, generic reference integration, upgrade fixtures |
| M5: beta candidate and handoff | Month 9 | Accessibility evidence, recovery exercise, release artifacts/SBOM, operator/maintainer guide, final upstream report |

Acceptance is based on published source, tests, reproducible artifacts, and explicit limitations—not claims of production certification or adoption.

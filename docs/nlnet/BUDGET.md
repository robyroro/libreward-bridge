## Payment evidence

Each request for payment should identify the public deliverables, repository revision, test evidence, documentation/report, and any upstream or audit links for that task. Upstream acceptance is desirable but not a payment dependency because it is outside the applicant's control; a published, tested and reviewable submission is the deliverable.

## Assumptions requiring applicant confirmation

- €60/hour accurately represents a cost-recovery rate rather than an unsubstantiated commercial rate.
- The applicant can provide 680 hours within the agreed eight-month period.
- An independent reviewer can perform the stated scope for approximately €6,000; obtain an indicative quote before the interactive review stage.
- The proposed work has no duplicate funding and cumulative prior NGI TALER support is below the programme limit.

# Roadmap

Completed feasibility evidence includes the generic API/worker model, tenant and operator boundaries, idempotent reward creation, single provider operation under concurrency, signed webhooks, liquidity/retention controls, mock end-to-end tests, and sanitized valueless GNU Taler sandbox evidence.

Future funded work:

- align the wallet RPC lifecycle and unknown-outcome recovery with GNU Taler upstream;
- build a reproducible valueless interoperability/failure-injection matrix;
- commission independent security and privacy review and remediate findings;
- mature the TypeScript SDK, generic reference integrator, OpenAPI conformance, and upgrade tooling;
- perform independent accessibility testing, deployment exercises, community onboarding, alpha/beta releases, and upstream feedback.

Real-money use remains out of scope until independent review and operator legal/treasury gates are complete.
